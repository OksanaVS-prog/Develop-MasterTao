Register cargo

• In order to start cooperation, it is necessary to register the cargo indicating information about the contents of the cargo, its cost and quantity. And also attaching the photo to the application.

• If it is necessary to check the conformity of the goods with the quantity, then when processing the goods on our site indicate this in the box "Note". This service is for a fee of 3 RMB per order. Any additional inspections of the cargo are discussed individually including their cost.

• Before shipping the goods to our warehouse in China, be sure to make a labeling, with your order number on our website, for each outer box so that we can identify exactly your cargo. If you can’t do it yourself, ask your supplier.

• After shipping the cargo to our warehouse in China, you must specify a track number for tracking parcels to China for each position in the order. You can enter the track number on the order page. The track number is another measure to identify your shipment in our warehouse.

• If you have processed the goods according to our instructions and have done everything correctly, when you receive your goods in a warehouse in China, the status of the order will change to "In stock".

• Upon receipt of the goods to the warehouse and change the status of the order, you will receive a notification by e-mail indicated during registration. The letter will include current information on the order, including date of receipt, order number and further steps to process it.
